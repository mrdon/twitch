Secrets to a delightful(!) PR process

Why does it take so much time?

What are pull requests?

1. Measure

You can't improve what you don't measure

2. Focus on the biggest bucket

Is it waiting, reviewing, or merging?

3. Only assign one person

Everyone's job means no ones job

4. Consider domain and tech expertise

One domain expert, one tech expert

5. Automate the small stuff

Stop the nitpicking comments on spacing!

6. Spec big things ahead of time

Reviews shouldn't be a surprise

7. Keep them small

Think "byte-sized" #dadjoke

8. Take it off line

Keep the heat out of the review

9. Automate deployment

Automation the end and work backwards

10. Automate merging

Ship when ready, not when you get around to it

Takeaway: PRs don't have to suck

A good PR process is the backbone of an elite team
